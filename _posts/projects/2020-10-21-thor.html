Science behind it!

The curious thing about a thor's hammer is that only a worthy person can pick it up. In this project, that worth person is, of course, me! 😁
when it is placed on metallic surface(like Iron) it will stick to it untill I touch it. The trick behind it is that - when it is placed on any surface the electromagnet inside the hammer gets active and this cause strong attractive force between hammer and metallic surface. I have a key with me which turns of the electromagnet of hammer when I touch and I will be able to pick it up very easily!
