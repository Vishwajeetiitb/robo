Realtime Vehicle tracker

A device for tracking E-shuttles in campus | Hobby project | Dec '19

Made tracking devices using NodeMCU, GPS Module and RF Module. Designed website to track these devices in realtime. Google's Snap to road Algorithm was integrated to accuracy of tracking on roads.
