About the Competition

This Competition was held during the 8th Inter IIT Tech Meet. During this meet, different tech competitions are conducted between IITs. One of them was the "DIC's Terrace Farming Challenge for Hilly areas". The task was to design an autonomous robot that can climb steps of 40cm and perform basic farming tasks like Cutting, Plucking, etc.
