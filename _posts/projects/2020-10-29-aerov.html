About Team Aerov

Team Aerov is a part of UMIC ( Umesh Mashruwal Innovation Cell ), IIT Bombay. This team focuses on Aerial robots related competition in worldwide. It has already participated in IARC Mission 7 and Barcelona Smart Drone challenge. Currently, it is focusing on IARC Mission 9. The detailed problem statement for IARC Mission 9 can be found here. Current major tasks are to travel 3 miles of distance and perform the task of replacing the communication module using the robotic arm on the drone

My contribution : Mechatronics Subsystem

My Current work is to design robotic arm with gripper for performing the tasks given in IARC Mission 9. I have made models for it and now working on optimizing its weight
