About Flipkart Grid 2.0 - Robotcs Challenge

During the pandemic period, Flipkart conducted an interesting competition for robotics enthusiasts under Flipkart Grid 2.0. There were three problem statements :

  • Intelligent Picking
  • Autonomous Indoor Drone
  • Autonomous Stair Climbing Robot.
We chose the Autonomous Indoor Drone Challenge in which we had to propose the solution of drone which will be able to carry 2kgs of load and able to navigate through a series of windows randomly placed in a room

Our Solution

I designed the cad model consisting of depth camera (for navigation and localization), computation unit (Rpi4) and bucket (to carry payload). The design was based on the contrains due to size of window through which it has to pass.
