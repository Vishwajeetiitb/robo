Flame sensing Drone

The Aeromodeling Club, IIT Bombay offered an interesting Challenge cum Hackathon in collaboration with Drona Aviation. Drona Aviation is a startup for drones. The Hackathon was based on those drones, we had to program the drones according to the problem statements offered by the Drona Aviation. There were three problem statements first was Flame Sensing Drone, Second was Wall following drone and last was height maintaining drone. We chose to take the Challenge of Flame Sensing Drone. In this challenge we had to code the drone in a way such that, drone will move away from the flame, whenenver it senses the flame
